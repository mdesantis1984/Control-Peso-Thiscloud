# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** February 25, 2026

## Introduction

Control Peso Thiscloud ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our weight tracking application (the "Service").

By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Information You Provide Directly

- **Google Account Information**: When you sign in with Google OAuth 2.0, we collect:
  - Name
  - Email address
  - Profile picture (optional)
  - Google User ID (for authentication purposes)

- **Weight and Health Data**: Information you voluntarily enter:
  - Weight measurements
  - Dates and times of measurements
  - Personal notes associated with measurements
  - Height, goal weight, starting weight (optional)
  - Date of birth (optional, for statistical purposes)

- **Preferences and Settings**:
  - Unit system preference (Metric/Imperial)
  - Language preference (English/Spanish)
  - Dark/Light theme preference
  - Notification settings
  - Time zone

### 2. Information Collected Automatically

- **Usage Data**: We collect information about how you interact with the Service:
  - Pages visited
  - Features used
  - Time spent on the application
  - Browser type and version
  - Device type and screen resolution
  - IP address (anonymized)

- **Cookies and Similar Technologies**:
  - Authentication cookies (session management)
  - Preference cookies (theme, language, settings)
  - Analytics cookies (via Google Analytics 4)

### 3. Information from Third Parties

- **Google OAuth**: When you authenticate with Google, we receive the information you authorize Google to share with us (name, email, profile picture).
- **Google Analytics**: We use Google Analytics 4 to understand how users interact with our Service. Google may collect additional data as described in [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

1. **Provide and Maintain the Service**:
   - Authenticate your identity
   - Store and display your weight data
   - Calculate statistics, trends, and projections
   - Save your preferences and settings

2. **Improve and Personalize the Service**:
   - Analyze usage patterns to improve features
   - Customize the user experience based on preferences
   - Develop new features based on user needs

3. **Communication**:
   - Send system notifications (if enabled)
   - Respond to your inquiries or support requests
   - Send important updates about the Service (rarely, only critical information)

4. **Security and Compliance**:
   - Detect and prevent fraudulent activity
   - Monitor for security threats
   - Comply with legal obligations

5. **Analytics and Research**:
   - Understand how users interact with the Service
   - Generate anonymized, aggregated statistics
   - Improve performance and user experience

## Data Storage and Security

### Storage Location

Your data is stored on our secure servers. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

- **Encryption**: Data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS.
- **Database Security**: Your data is stored in a secure SQL Server database with access controls and encryption at rest.
- **Authentication**: We use Google OAuth 2.0 for secure authentication. We do NOT store passwords.
- **Access Control**: Only authorized personnel have access to the infrastructure, and access is logged and monitored.

### Data Retention

- **Active Accounts**: We retain your data as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service.
- **Account Deletion**: If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.
- **Backups**: Backup copies may be retained for up to 30 days for disaster recovery purposes.

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

1. **With Your Consent**: We may share your information if you explicitly authorize us to do so.

2. **Service Providers**: We may share information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating the Service:

   These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for the purposes we specify.

3. **Legal Requirements**: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid legal requests (e.g., court orders, subpoenas).

4. **Protection of Rights**: We may disclose information to protect our rights, property, or safety, or the rights, property, or safety of our users or the public.

5. **Business Transfers**: If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you of any such change.
